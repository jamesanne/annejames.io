About

I have a background in Cultural Anthropology and twenty years building GTM for B2B SaaS companies — including Run:ai (acquired by NVIDIA), Sage Business Cloud, and a handful of YC-backed startups.

I came to marketing through fieldwork: studying how people make meaning, form groups, and decide who to trust. It turns out buyers work the same way.

Technical founders hire me when they've realized marketing is a science, not a vibe. The anthropologist framing isn't a cute metaphor — it's the actual methodology.
